Who am I?

My name is Jaime, and I'm passionate about programming. After completing my University internship in a technology company in Madrid, I embarked on a new adventure and traveled to Miami, where I studied an intensive Web Development Bootcamp for several months.

After this great experience, I traveled back to Madrid where I started my professional career as a Frontend Developer. Since then, every day I seek to acquire new knowledge, from Web Design principles to Artificial Intelligence.

I am a very active and persistent person, who learns quickly and who takes every opportunity to learn something new.
