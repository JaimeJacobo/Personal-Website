Sobre Mí

Who am I?

My name is Jaime, and coding is my passion. After completing my internship in a technology company back in Spain, where I discovered my love for coding, I decided to make a full change in my life. I parted from my family and friends and moved to a new country with a new language to pursue my dream of learning and growing as a web developer and as a web designer.

Creativity and adaptation are two of my biggest strengths, but I am also dedicated, practical, persistent and I pay close attention to details.

I am a fast learner who sees an opportunity to learn and acquire knowledge in every problem or conflict I encounter.

What do I like to do?

Programming aside, my two big passions are sports and music. I have been playing all kinds of sports since I was a little kid. If I had to choose two sports, they would be skateboarding and football.

Regarding music, I love listening to it everywhere I go. From a big music festival with my friends to putting my headphones on when I’m coding; every moment is a perfect opportunity to listen to some music.

CS Dojo

¿Quién soy?

Mi nombre es Jaime, y me apasiona la programación. Después de realizar mis prácticas universitarias en una compañía tecnológica en Madrid, decidí embarcarme en un nueva aventura y viajar a Miami, donde estudié un bootcamp intensivo de Desarrollo Web durante varios meses.

Después de esta gran experiencia, viajé de vuelta a Madrid donde empecé mi trayectoria profesional como Frontend Developer. Desde entonces, cada día busco adquirir nuevos conocimientos, desde principios de diseño web hasta Inteligencia Artificial.

Soy una persona muy activa y persistente, que aprende rápido y que aprovecha cada oportunidad para aprender algo nuevo.

¿Qué me guta hacer?

Las dos tecnologías que más disfruto estudiando, y a las que dedico la mayor parte de mi tiempo, son JavaScript y Python. No obstante, me llaman mucho la atención otra serie de lenguajes como Java, C++, etc., sobre los cuales voy indagando poco a poco.

Programación aparte, mis dos grandes pasiones son el deporte y la música. He practicado todo tipo de deportes desde niño. Si tuviese que elegir dos deportes, elegiría el fútbol americano y el skateboarding.

Sobre la música, disfruto enormemente escucharla en cada momento. Desde un festival de música con mis amigos hasta ponerme los cascos cuando estoy programando; cada momento es una excelente oportunidad para escuchar algo de música.

¿De dónde aprendo?

I was born in Mexico, but I have lived in Spain for the last 12 years. After studying for a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology back in Madrid, I decided to make a change in my life and move to Miami to begin a whole new adventure.

When I think of my future, I see myself living in a place where I can keep growing as a developer, as a designer and as a person.

My Projects

Let's Go Florida

Let's Go Florida is a web application that provides information about hotels, restaurants and activities of any city in Florida. (JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, Photoshop, Node.js)

MyLy Quotes

MyLy Quotes is a web application where you can upload, edit and delete your favorite music quotes.
(JavaScript, HTML, CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap, Node.js, MongoDB)

GitHub Searcher

GitHub Searcher is an application where you can get information about any user's repositories. (JavaScript, Direct DOM Manipulation, GitHub's API, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap)

IronJack

Ironjack is a Blackjack version game, a comparing card game that uses combinatorics and probability to determine the winner. (JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery)
