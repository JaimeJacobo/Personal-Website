About Me

Who am I?

I come from Spain, where I studied Psychology in Madrid. After I did my intership in a technology company, I decided to aim my life towards a new passion: programming.
I began a bootcamp focused on JavaScript and Web Development in Miami. After finishing it, my desire to keep learning about this amazing world kept growing.

What do I like to do?

Programming aside, my two big passions are sports and music. I have been playing all kinds of sports since I was a little kid. If I had to chose 2 sports, I would pick skateboarding and football.
About music, I love listening to music everywhere I go. From a big music festival with my friends to putting my headphones on when I'm coding. Every moment is perfect to listen to some music.

Where do I come from?

I was born in Mexico, but I have lived in Spain for the last 12 years. After studying Psychology in Madrid, I decided to make a change in my life and move to Miami to begin a whole new adventure. When I think of my future, I see myself living in a place where I feel comfortable with myself, where I can keep growing as a developer, as a designer and as a person.
